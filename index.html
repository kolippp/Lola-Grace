Biography

Lola Grace is a dynamic and versatile performer specializing in dance, aerial arts, and contortion. With over a decade of training in contemporary, jazz, and ballet, she combines strong technical foundation with expressive artistry and stage presence.

Lola has performed professionally in both stage and commercial environments. She is currently featured as a dancer in Royal Caribbean’s production shows aboard Quantum of the Seas, delivering high-energy performances in large-scale theatrical productions. Prior to this, she performed at Universal Orlando in the DreamWorks Imagination Celebration, gaining valuable experience in live entertainment and character-driven performance.

In addition to her dance career, Lola is an accomplished aerialist and contortionist. She has worked with multiple entertainment companies including ESU Entertainment, Aerial Dragons, Odd-O-T’s Entertainment, and Cirque Vertigo. Her aerial repertoire includes lyra, straps, harness work, and flying pole, showcasing a unique combination of strength, flexibility, and control.

Lola’s performance background is complemented by her work as a teacher and choreographer, having trained and mentored dancers at institutions such as United Ballet Theatre and Dehner Elite Arts in Central Florida. She has also been involved in competitive and commercial dance projects, including Dancing With The Local Stars in North Carolina and campaigns with Dancelook.

Her training includes a full scholarship to the prestigious Joffrey Ballet School in New York City, where she completed an intensive program in contemporary and jazz. She further developed her skills at Xtreme Dance Studio and Anti-Gravity Orlando, expanding into aerial disciplines and performance techniques.

Driven, disciplined, and continuously evolving, Lola Grace brings a unique blend of elegance, athleticism, and versatility to every stage she steps on.
